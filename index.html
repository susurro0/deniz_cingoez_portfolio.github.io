Operating Principles

Management Standards (Live Artifacts) Engineering Rubric → ADR Framework →

Autonomous Teams by Design

Measure success by decision ownership. Teams operate with clear boundaries, explicit interfaces, and documented decision rights (ADRs).

Paved Paths, Not PowerPoints

Reduce cognitive load through standardized pipelines, templates, and golden paths—defaults that scale quality without slowing delivery.

Governance Built Into the Platform

Embed security, compliance, and AI safety directly into tooling, not review processes or manual gates.

Architecture as a Leadership Tool

Use system design to clarify tradeoffs, align stakeholders, and constrain complexity before it becomes organizational debt.

Business Outcomes First

Prioritize initiatives that move financial, operational, or risk metrics—engineering excellence is a means, not the goal.

Observability as a Standard

Systems must be transparent. We prioritize structured logging, distributed tracing, and real-time SLIs to move from reactive firefighting to proactive engineering.
